Stephen Hawking

Stephen Hawking on the left with his wife

Stephen Hawking's life, but long story short.

  • 1942: Born in Oxford, England (300 years after the death of Galileo).
  • 1962: Graduates with a first class degree in natural sciences.
  • 1963: He is diagnosed with an "incurable disease" at the age of 21.
  • 1973: In the same year he discovers, that black holes could leak energy and particles into space.
  • 1988: Publishes A Brief History of Time: From the Big Bang to Black Holes.
  • 1993: Publishes Black Holes and Baby Universes, and other Essay.
  • 2004: Hawking announces that he has solved the Black Hole paradox.
  • 2012: Hawking is awarded the Fundamental Physics Prize.
  • 2014: Hawking warns about the dangers of artificial intelligence.
  • 2018: Hawking's family confirmed he had died peacefully.
